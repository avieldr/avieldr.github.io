AyyekaGo

Participating in the Field Asset Intelligent Platform team, I led the development of ‘AyyekaGo’ - a mobile app that communicates with Ayyeka’s IOT devices on BLE, developed on React native for both android and ios.

My area of responsibilities included design, development, maintenance, testing and deployment.

The AyyekaGo mobile app enables quick and easy installation and enhanced on-site maintenance of the Wavelet device.

Available on iOS and Android, this user-friendly app displays the cellular network operator being used, type of network (4G, 3G, 2G), signal strength, and connection and transmission times to Ayyeka’s data hub, as well as current power sources and their status (internal battery, external power, etc.). On site, AyyekaGo easily and securely connects to the Wavelet via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), allowing for direct control, such as immediate transmission of Wavelet data to the server, on-demand sensor sampling, and more.
